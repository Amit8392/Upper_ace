Tailored Transportation Solutions

At Upper Ace Logistics, we offer a diverse range of freight carrier services designed to meet the unique demands of businesses of all sizes. Whether you need to move a full truckload across the continent or a smaller, time-sensitive shipment, our experienced team and modern fleet are ready to provide a seamless and secure transportation experience. Explore our services below to find the perfect fit for your logistics needs.

Full Truckload (FTL) Shipping

When your shipment requires an entire trailer, our Full Truckload (FTL) service is the most efficient and direct option. Ideal for large volumes of goods, dedicated routes, or time-sensitive deliveries, FTL minimizes handling and transit time, reducing the risk of damage and ensuring your cargo reaches its destination quickly and safely.

  • Dedicated Trailer: Exclusively for your goods.
  • Faster Transit: Direct routes, minimal stops.
  • Reduced Handling: Lower risk of damage.
  • Ideal for: Large, high-value, or urgent shipments.
Get an FTL Quote

Less Than Truckload (LTL) Shipping

For smaller shipments that don't require a full trailer, our Less Than Truckload (LTL) service provides a cost-effective solution. Your cargo shares trailer space with other shipments, allowing you to pay only for the space you use. Upper Ace Logistics excels at consolidating LTL freight efficiently, ensuring your goods are transported safely and economically.

  • Cost-Effective: Pay only for your occupied space.
  • Flexible: Accommodates smaller shipment sizes.
  • Network Efficiency: Utilizes a broad carrier network.
  • Ideal for: Smaller, non-urgent shipments.
Get an LTL Quote

Cross-Border Freight (Canada-US)

Navigating the complexities of cross-border logistics between Canada and the United States can be challenging. Upper Ace Logistics has extensive experience and expertise in cross-border transportation, including customs clearance, documentation, and regulatory compliance. We ensure a smooth and hassle-free transition for your cargo across the border, minimizing delays and maximizing efficiency.

  • Customs Expertise: Smooth border crossings.
  • Compliance Guaranteed: Adherence to all regulations.
  • Reduced Delays: Efficient documentation processing.
  • Routes: Connecting BC, Canada to key US destinations.
