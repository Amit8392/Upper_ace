About Upper Ace Logistics

We're more than just a trucking company; we're your reliable partner.

Our Story: Building Trust, One Shipment at a Time

Since our inception, Upper Ace Logistics has been dedicated to providing exceptional freight carrier services across Canada and the United States. Founded on the principles of reliability, efficiency, and unwavering commitment to our clients, we've grown from a vision into a trusted name in the logistics industry. We understand that your cargo isn't just goods; it's your business, your promises, and your livelihood. That's why we don't just carry freight; we carry your trust.

Efficiency

Streamlining processes and optimizing routes for timely deliveries.

Customer Focus

Tailoring solutions and providing responsive service to meet client needs.

Our Team: The Heart of Our Operations

Behind every successful delivery is a team of dedicated professionals. From our experienced and rigorously trained drivers to our meticulous dispatchers and responsive customer service agents, every member of the Upper Ace Logistics family is committed to excellence. We invest in our people, ensuring they have the skills, training, and support to handle your most challenging logistics requirements with precision and care.

Our drivers are more than just operators; they're the face of our company on the road, upholding our promise of trust and reliability.

Modern Fleet & Advanced Technology

At Upper Ace Logistics, we pride ourselves on maintaining a modern, well-equipped fleet. Our trucks undergo regular, stringent maintenance checks to ensure maximum reliability and safety on every journey. We also leverage cutting-edge logistics technology, including advanced GPS tracking and Transport Management Systems (TMS), to provide real-time visibility and optimized routing. This commitment to innovation means greater efficiency and peace of mind for you.

We're constantly updating our equipment and software to meet evolving industry standards and client demands.

Commitment to Safety & Compliance

Safety isn't just a buzzword at Upper Ace Logistics; it's the cornerstone of our operations. We adhere to the highest industry safety standards and regulatory compliance, including all provincial (BC) and federal transportation laws for both Canada and the US. Our drivers receive ongoing training in defensive driving, cargo securement, and hazardous materials handling (if applicable). We are fully insured, giving you complete confidence that your valuable cargo is protected every step of the way.
